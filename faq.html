How should I prepare my packaging for return?
If you no longer have the original packaging, you can return your order in any other packaging, as long as it is well sealed to prevent the items from being lost. It is not necessary to write any shipping address on the package. Don't forget to include your purchase receipt.
What are the conditions for making a return?
If the purchase made did not meet your expectations, we offer the possibility of making a return. Items must be in their original condition and have all tags. It is necessary to make the return in the same market/region in which you made the purchase.
What are the deadlines for exchanges and returns?
Online purchase: you have 30 days from the date your order was shipped to receive a refund in a VALE EXCHANGE. If you wish to receive it in the original payment method, you have 7 days from receipt of the order to make a return. Purchase in a physical store: you have 30 days from the date of purchase.
Can I make a return in a physical store?
Yes, you can return your items free of charge to any of our Cara stores. The option to return in store is available for both online and in-store purchases. You must only present the items for return and the purchase receipt.
Can I return items from different orders in a single shipment?
Please note that it is not possible to return items from different orders together. Therefore, you will have to request a return for each order and send it separately.
